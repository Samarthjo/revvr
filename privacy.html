privacy policy

last updated: june 2026. short version: we collect very little, use it only for what we say, and never sell it.

what we collect

Email addresses — only if you sign up for the free cheat sheet or newsletter. collected via Web3Forms and stored securely. used only for revvr communications.

Contact form messages — if you use the contact form, your message and email are sent to us via Web3Forms. we use this only to reply to you.

Page view data — we use basic analytics (no personal tracking, no cookies for advertising) to understand which guides are useful. nothing that identifies you personally.

what we don't do

  • we don't sell your email to anyone. ever.
  • we don't show you ads based on your browsing behaviour.
  • we don't share your data with bike manufacturers, dealers, or any third party.
  • we don't use tracking pixels or ad retargeting.

third-party links

revvr links out to ZigWheels, BikeDekho, BikeWale, and Team-BHP for full reviews and price data. these sites have their own privacy policies and data practices — we have no control over them.
